Our Process

A systematic approach to identifying and implementing effective energy-saving solutions.

Our 7-Step Approach

From Audit to Implementation

1
Preliminary Audit

A quick, low-cost assessment to identify key areas of energy waste and potential savings.

2
Detailed Data Collection

In-depth measurements, data logging, and analysis of all major energy-consuming systems.

3
Energy Analysis & Benchmarking

Comparative study against industry benchmarks to establish an effective energy baseline.

4
Opportunity Identification

Pinpointing specific, actionable energy-saving measures (ESMs) and their potential impact.

5
Implementation Strategy

Developing a prioritized action plan with clear timelines and resource allocation for ESMs.

6
Project Management & Execution

Overseeing the seamless installation and commissioning of the energy-saving projects.

7

Verification & Monitoring

Measuring, verifying, and reporting the actual savings achieved, followed by continuous monitoring.
